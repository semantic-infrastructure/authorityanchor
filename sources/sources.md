# Sources

## Purpose
This page documents the authoritative institutional references underpinning authority, provenance, and trust anchoring for digital systems.
The project focuses on verifiable authority signals, not branding, reputation, or identity marketing.
This page is reference-only and non-commercial.

## Source hierarchy
1. Primary sources: international standards and formal specifications
2. Secondary sources: public-sector security and trust frameworks
3. Excluded sources: branding, reputation scoring, marketing models
